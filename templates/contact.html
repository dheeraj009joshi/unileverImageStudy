Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with creating my first study?

Sign up for a free account, then use our step-by-step wizard to design your study. The system will guide you through each step of the process.

What types of studies can I create?

Our platform supports both image-based and text-based studies with customizable rating scales, classification questions, and IPED parameters.

How do I share my study with participants?

Once your study is created, you'll receive a unique share link and QR code that you can distribute to your participants.

Can I export my study data?

Yes! You can export your data in multiple formats including JSON and CSV for further analysis in statistical software.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard security measures and never collect personal information from participants without explicit consent.

What is RDE?

Rule Developing Experimentation (RDE) is a sophisticated research methodology that enables researchers to efficiently estimate individual-level parameters through carefully designed experimental tasks. Our system provides a comprehensive platform for creating, managing, and analyzing RDE studies.
